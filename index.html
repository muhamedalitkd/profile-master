About Me

I'm a

I am a Software Engineer, an athlete and a content maker. I have started coding since I joined Asia Pacific University in 2017 after high school. With the interest of developing responsive websites, I really wanted to know how to make mobile web-applications. So, I have joined the Centre of Technology and Innovation company and started to gain knowledge & experience in Ionic, TypeScript and working with API's.

Beside it, I have been doing combat sports since my childhood starting with Taekwon-Do ITF at the age of 8. I have managed to achieve a black belt 1st Dan and becoming a member of National Team for Kyrgyzstan.
Moreover, I have been competing in different types of sport such as Boxing, Muay-Thai.

And lastly, I am creating a content about sports on my YouTube channel.
I decided that combination of my interest in coding and sports would bring to the world something that could help people. So, I decided to develop a software providing a content of physical exercises where everyone will be able to learn self-defense, cutting weight, strengthen muscle joints, increase stamina. But most importantly, becoming a physically stronger as well as mentally.
